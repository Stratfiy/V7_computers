Who We Are

One Stop Solutions For All Your IT Needs

V7 Computers is a comprehensive Information Technology solutions provider based in Hosur, Tamil Nadu, with a presence across Krishnagiri and Bangalore. We specialise in retail, corporate, and online IT integration.

Led by M. Vijay Kumar B.E, Managing Director, our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering the latest technology paired with best-in-class solutions tailored to our customers' unique needs.

As an authorised dealer for leading brands including Dell, Lenovo, HP, Acer, Asus, Canon, Epson, CP Plus, and Hikvision, we ensure every product we sell is 100% genuine with official warranty.
